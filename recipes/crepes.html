Crepes




Description

French style crepes with nutella, perfect for a dessert.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the flour and eggs
  2. Gradually add the milt and the water
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides
  6. When done, at Nutella to taste
