Soup




Description

Homemade chicken soup, ideal for cold weather.

Ingredients

  • Chiken breast
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Soup pasta
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken and the vegetables together for 45 mins
  2. Take the vegetables off
  3. Filter the boiled soup
  4. add the already cooked chicken in the filtered soup
  5. Add pasta and seasoning to taste, boil for 7-8 mins
  6. Serve
